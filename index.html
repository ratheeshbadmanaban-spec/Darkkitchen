HomeKarupatti, also known as Palm Jaggery, is a traditional natural sweetener made from palm tree sap. Unlike refined sugar, it is chemical-free, nutrient-rich, and packed with health benefits. Our mission is to bring back this age-old superfood to modern kitchens in the purest form. AboutKarupatti is a natural sweetener made from the sap of palm trees. It is considered a healthier alternative to refined sugar. The sap is collected from the palm tree, boiled in large vessels until it thickens, and then cooled down to form solid jaggery. It has a dark brown to blackish color, which is why it is called Karupatti. Slightly bitter yet sweet in taste, it is rich in essential nutrients such as calcium, iron, magnesium, and potassium.
